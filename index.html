Triple Layer Cheesecake

⏱ 1h 33m ⭐ 4.8 Dessert

A rich, creamy cheesecake with three indulgent layers of flavor — smooth, sweet, and perfectly balanced.

Ingredients

  • 3 packages cream cheese
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/3 cup sour cream
  • Vanilla, flour, salt
  • Butterscotch & chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix cream cheese and sugar until smooth.
  3. Divide batter into 3 flavored layers.
  4. Bake for 55–60 minutes.
  5. Cool & refrigerate 3 hours.

Recipe Info

Difficulty: Medium

Servings: 8–10
