Triple Layer Cheesecake

A rich, creamy cheesecake with three indulgent layers of flavor — smooth, sweet, and perfectly balanced for any dessert lover.

Ingredients

  • 3 packages cream cheese (8 oz each), softened
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/3 cup sour cream
  • 3 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup butterscotch chips, melted
  • 1–1.5 cups vanilla wafer crumbs

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Prepare crust.
  2. Beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add eggs, sour cream, flour, vanilla, and salt.
  3. Divide batter into three bowls and mix each layer (butterscotch, chocolate, white creme).
  4. Pour layers over crust and bake 55–60 minutes.
  5. Cool completely, then refrigerate for at least 3 hours.
  6. Optional: add triple drizzle before serving.
